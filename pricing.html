Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to decide with confidence.

Can I change my plan later? expand_more

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time from your dashboard. If you upgrade mid-cycle, we'll pro-rate the difference automatically.

What happens if I exceed my lead limit? expand_more

We'll notify you at 80% and 100% of your limit. You can purchase additional lead bundles or upgrade to the next tier — we'll never cut off your campaigns mid-month.

Is there a free trial available? expand_more

Yes! You can try the Growth plan features free for 14 days — no credit card required. No risk, just results.

What does the "Growth" plan include vs Starter? expand_more

Growth unlocks multi-step drip campaigns, team collaboration with shared notes and pipeline, CRM integration (Google/Outlook), custom sales funnels, and a full revenue analytics dashboard. It's designed for teams of 3–5 people who want a predictable, repeatable sales process.

What makes Enterprise ("The Partner") different? expand_more

Enterprise gives you a fully white-labelled solution under your brand, a dedicated account manager, custom security controls, SSO, unlimited seats, and custom AI training on your proprietary data. It is a bespoke partnership, not just software.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits or startups? expand_more

Yes — we support organizations making a difference. Reach out to our team with your documentation and we'll arrange a 30% discount on any paid plan.
